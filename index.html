About me.

Hello and welcome to my resume. My name is Loïc Pennequin and I am (javascript-obsessed) fullstack web developer.

After spending a few years learning foreign languages and other non web-related stuff, I discovered the joy of coding through POP School Lens and I haven't been able to let go of my computer ever since (Well, that was already the case before, but you know what I'm saying).

I thoroughly enjoy creating pixel-perfect interfaces with intelligent design/user experiences. Although I have more affinities with front-end development, I also find building back end solutions to my clients' needs very interesting. Nothing more satisfying to create a new project by yourself from A to Z, right ? Don't worry however, I have absolutely no problem with working as a team and I will do my best to be the most accomodating and useful to any project or task I am given.
